自己紹介 / about me

こんにちは！ My name is jaceYUP (or just Jace, if you prefer). I'm 19 years old and I live in Texas, USA. I made this little nook of the internet primarily for sharing my music, but I also like to share updates and posts about current happenings. Thank you for stopping by! It really means a lot, so... ありがとう！('ω')ノ

Q & A

なんで日本語を使いますか？ / Why do you use Japanese?
So long ago, I first discovered the anime Gurren Lagann from a fanmade edit on YouTube. At the time, I was about five-years-old. Since then, I've passively endulged in Japanese popular culture, but it wasn't until roughly three years ago that I became very interested in the Japanese music scene, largely thanks to Revo, the lead of Sound Horizon. I want to master the Japanese language so that I can better understand the music culture there and replicate the style.
What do you want to do with this site?
I want to use this site to express myself, share my music, and portray my thoughts.
